About the company

Deviro services (PTY) Ltd is a private company founded by Ezrah Maluleke in 2019 and we are hundred-percent black ownership. We specialize in professional landscape architecture and horticulture. Our team carries various experts with over decades work experience for the required landscape and construction services. We are situated in Tshwane and has been in service both international and local. As a company that is registered with South African Council for the Landscape Architectural Profession (SACLAP) and we thrive to design and install what is required by our clients.
