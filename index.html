WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO BEST

Deviro services (PTY) L.td is a private company founded by Ezrah Maluleke in 2019 and we are hundred-percent black ownership. We specialize in professional landscape architecture and horticulture. Our team carries various experts with over decades work experience for the required landscape and construction services. We are situated in Tshwane and has been in service both international and local. As a company that is registered with South African Council for the Landscape Architectural Profession (SACLAP) and we thrive to design and install what is required by our clients.

Horticulture and Maintenance

We offer erosion control methods on construction sites or as an alternative to the traditional process of seed broadcasting. It is very effective on steep slopes and sloping lawns. The advantage of hydroseeding is that large areas can be completed in very short timeframes with quick germination results. Should you require a different vegetation than grass as an end-result, we have a variety of low water requirement plants/groundcovers/creepers that can be used as alternative.
